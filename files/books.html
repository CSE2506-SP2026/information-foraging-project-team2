Books

Recommended reads that support positive discipline, emotional development, and practical tools for everyday parenting.


Emotional Muscle: Strong Parents, Strong Children

Kerry Kelly Novick & Jack Novick, PhD

A practical guide to building emotional resilience—helping children manage feelings, handle frustration, and develop healthy independence through connected parenting.

No-Drama Discipline

Daniel J. Siegel & Tina Payne Bryson

Explains the brain science behind behavior and offers calm, effective discipline strategies that strengthen relationships while setting firm limits.

Books by Becky Bailey

Multiple titles

A collection of approachable, classroom-tested resources focused on self-regulation, connection, and positive guidance for young children.

Conscious Discipline

Becky A. Bailey, PhD

A framework that teaches adults how to stay regulated first—so kids can learn to regulate too—using routines, language, and connection-based discipline.

Easy to Love, Difficult to Discipline

Becky A. Bailey, PhD

Helps parents replace power struggles with clear boundaries and empathy, supporting cooperation and emotional growth.

I Love You Rituals

Becky A. Bailey, PhD

Short connection-building routines that strengthen attachment, ease transitions, and support emotional security.

There Has Got to be a Better Way

Becky A. Bailey, PhD

Focuses on real-life discipline moments and how to respond with composure and intention, building skills instead of escalating conflict.
